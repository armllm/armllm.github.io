Highlights

The first LLM Summer School in Armenia brought together over 70 students and researchers for an intensive week of learning and collaboration. The program featured lectures, hands-on workshops, and practical sessions where participants applied their knowledge to innovative projects.

The curriculum covered a range of both core and specialized topics in LLMs, including Transformer architectures, pretraining, model alignment, parameter-efficient tuning, in-context learning and reasoning, multimodality and Vision LLMs, LLM interpretability, safety & privacy, knowledge graphs, and retrieval augmented generation (RAG).

The event was co-hosted by the American University of Armenia and YerevaNN, with the lectures taking place at the AUA campus. Nebius AI and Picsart provided generous support for the summer school.
