Building Armenia’s LLM talent pipeline
with a world-class annual summer school

The 3rd edition (Aug 3–7, 2026) brings together students, researchers, and engineers for lectures, practical sessions, and a 24-hour hack day (Aug 8) with our partner AI9.

What we deliver (every year)

• High-signal lectures on modern LLM training, post-training, and safety
• Hands-on practice (tools, evaluation, workflows) + collaborative learning
• Hackathon to convert learning into prototypes and community projects
• Access + inclusion via fee waivers for students and exceptional applicants
We’re seeking donor support to expand scholarships, improve hybrid delivery, and sustain compute + operations for 2026.

Call to action

We’re seeking donor support for 2026

Our goal is to expand access (waivers), strengthen the hybrid experience, and sustainably run an annual program that keeps Armenia’s AI talent connected to frontier work.

Where funds go

• Scholarships / fee waivers for students and exceptional applicants
• Compute + infrastructure for hands-on labs (in-person track)
• Speaker support (travel/hosting where needed)
• Venue + operations (staffing, materials, logistics)
• Hybrid delivery (streaming, recordings, tooling)
We can share a detailed budget breakdown and impact targets tailored to your giving preferences.

Sponsorship options (examples)

• Scholarship sponsor: underwrite fee waivers for a cohort
• Compute sponsor: support GPU access for practical sessions
• Program sponsor: support operations + hybrid delivery

Access policy (2026)

• In-person: 100,000 AMD (includes hands-on + GPU access)
• Online: 200 USD (theory sessions; limited aid based on funding)
• Student waivers: up to 90% • Armenian university students: reduced fee (~10,000 AMD)
• Employer payments supported (prepayment / tax invoice workflows)

Recognition

• Logo on website and materials
• Acknowledgement in opening/closing sessions
• Optional joint announcement / media mention
