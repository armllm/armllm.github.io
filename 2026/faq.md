### üß≠ 1) Program Overview

**When and where is the summer school happening?** The 2026 school is scheduled for **August 3-7, 2026** at the **AI9 Startup Campus** (9 Isahakyan St, Yerevan, Armenia).

**Is there an online option?** Yes! We offer an online track via Zoom. This covers all the theoretical sessions. However, please note that online participants typically do not get access to GPU resources.

**Will there be a hackathon?** Yes. The hackathon will start on **August 8 at 00:00** and run for a full **24-hour hack day**. It will be organized by our partner **AI9**. Armenia LLM Summer School will act as a **content partner** for the hackathon.

**What should I bring?** A **laptop** is a must-have! All the hands-on and practical sessions require you to have your own machine.

**Do I need a powerful computer?** If you are attending in person, we provide the **GPU resources** for the heavy lifting. You just need a reliable laptop to connect and code. Online participants generally do not have access to these shared GPU resources.

### ‚úÖ 2) Eligibility & Technical Background

**Who should apply?** The program is designed for motivated learners in AI, including students, researchers, and engineers. If you are interested in LLMs and can follow technical sessions, you are encouraged to apply.

**Do I need prior LLM experience?** No. Prior hands-on LLM experience is not required. However, applicants are expected to have a foundational understanding of machine learning and neural networks.

**Do I need Python experience?** Python proficiency is expected and is explicitly asked in the form. Applicants are asked to self-report their level (e.g., excellent, somewhat proficient, or not proficient).

### üìù 3) Application & Selection

**How are applicants selected?** Selection is competitive and follows a two-stage process: (1) initial form application and (2) technical assessment. Final confirmations are sent after evaluating both the application and the test results.

**What information do I need for registration?** The form asks for personal/contact details, location, academic/industry affiliation, experience with ML and LLMs, Python proficiency, and a motivation paragraph (~100 words). You may also provide links such as GitHub and Google Scholar.

**What files do I need to upload?** You must upload your CV/resume (1 file, up to 10 MB).

**What is the application timeline for 2026?**
- **Stage 1 (Initial application):** submit the form and CV by **May 15, 2026**.
- **Stage 2 (Technical assessment):** applicants should monitor email for a test link on **June 10** (after the application phase closes on June 1).
- **Final step:** successful candidates receive confirmation by email.

**What if I do not receive the technical test link?** Contact the organizers at **armeniallm@gmail.com**.

**Is there a waitlist?** Since capacity is limited and selection is competitive, applicants may not all be admitted. If in-person spots open, we may contact waitlisted applicants; in some cases, online participation may be offered.

**Can international applicants register?** Yes. International applicants are welcome; payment and logistics instructions are shared after acceptance.

### üí∏ 4) Fees, Financial Aid & Payments

**How much does the program cost?**

- **In-Person (General Participation):** 100,000 AMD (or equivalent in other currencies). This covers all theoretical and practical sessions, including **GPU access**.
- **Online Participation:** 200 USD (or equivalent). This covers the theoretical sessions. The aid for online participation will be distributed based on funding available.

**Is there financial aid for students?** Yes, we want to make this accessible!

- **Student Waivers:** We offer financial aid applications for students that can cover up to **90% of the fee**.
- **University Students:** Students currently enrolled in universities often qualify for a significantly reduced fee (around 10,000 AMD).
- **International/Exceptional Applicants:** If we have the budget, we sometimes offer waivers (from 50% to full scholarships) to non-student applicants with exceptional backgrounds.

**Can I apply for financial aid during registration?** Yes. The form includes a direct question asking whether you are applying for financial aid.

**How do I pay if I am an international applicant?** International bank transfers. If those do not work for you, let us know if you are stuck.

**Can my company pay for me?** Absolutely. We can issue a **prepayment invoice** for your employer to process. We can also provide an official tax invoice after the program ends for your reimbursement.

**Will I get payment documents for my university or employer?** Yes. If needed, we can provide prepayment and/or tax invoice documents for reimbursement workflows.

### üéì 5) Participation, Certification & Policies

**Will I get a certificate?** Yes! Everyone who fully attends the sessions, whether in-person or online, receives an official **Certificate of Participation**.

**Can I get a refund if I cannot attend?** If you let us know early enough, we can usually issue a refund so we can offer your spot to someone on the waitlist.
